GeoImage

  • GeoImage[reg] gives a satellite image of the geo region reg.
  • GeoImage[reg,mapstyle] gives an image of the geo region reg with style mapstyle.

Attributes:Protected, ReadProtected

Options: AlignmentPoint, BaselinePosition, ColorSpace, GeoCenter, GeoModel, GeoProjection, GeoRange, GeoRangePadding, GeoServer, GeoZoomLevel, ImageResolution, ImageSize, Interleaving, Magnification, MetaInformation, Method
