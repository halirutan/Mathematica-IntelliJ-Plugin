Moment

  • Moment[list,r] gives the rth sample moment of the elements in list.
  • Moment[dist,r] gives the rth moment of the symbolic distribution dist.
  • Moment[ … ,{r1,r2, … }] gives the {r1,r2, … }th multivariate moment.
  • Moment[r] represents the rth formal moment.

Attributes: NHoldAll, Protected, ReadProtected

Symbol has no options.
