NIntegrate

  • NIntegrate[f,{x,xmin,xmax}] gives a numerical approximation to the integral ∫ xmin xmax f d ​ x.
  • NIntegrate[f,{x,xmin,xmax},{y,ymin,ymax}, … ] gives a numerical approximation to the multiple integral ∫ xmin xmax dx ∫ ymin ymax d ​ y … f.

Attributes: HoldAll, Protected

Options: AccuracyGoal, Compiled, EvaluationMonitor, Exclusions, MaxPoints, MaxRecursion, Method, MinRecursion, PrecisionGoal, WorkingPrecision
