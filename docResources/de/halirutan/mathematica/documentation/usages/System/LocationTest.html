LocationTest

  • LocationTest[data] tests whether the mean or median of the data is zero.
  • LocationTest[{data1,data2}] tests whether the means or medians of data1 and data2 are equal.
  • LocationTest[dspec, μ 0] tests a location measure against μ 0.
  • LocationTest[dspec, μ 0,"property"] returns the value of "property".

Attributes: Protected, ReadProtected

Options: AlternativeHypothesis, Method, SignificanceLevel, MaxIterations, VerifyTestAssumptions
