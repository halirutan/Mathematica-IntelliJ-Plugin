SystemsModelDelete

  • SystemsModelDelete[sys,{in1, … }] deletes the subsystem of the systems model sys associated with inputs at position ini.
  • SystemsModelDelete[sys,{in1, … },{out1, … }] also deletes the subsystem associated with outputs at positions outi.
  • SystemsModelDelete[sys,{in1, … },{out1, … },{s1, s2, … }] deletes the subsystem of the state-space model sys associated with inputs, outputs, and states at ini, outi, and si, respectively.

Attributes: Protected, ReadProtected

Symbol has no options.
