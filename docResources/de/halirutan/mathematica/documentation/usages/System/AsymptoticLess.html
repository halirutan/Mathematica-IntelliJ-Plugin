AsymptoticLess

  • AsymptoticLess[f,g,x → x*] gives conditions for f(x) ≺ g(x) or f(x) ∈ o(g(x)) as x → x*.
  • AsymptoticLess[f,g,{x1, … ,xn} → {x 1 * , … ,x n * }] gives conditions for f(x1, … ,xn) ≺ g(x1, … ,xn) or f(x1, … ,xn) ∈ o(g(x1, … ,xn)) as {x1, … ,xn} → {x 1 * , … ,x n * }.

Attributes:Protected

Options: Analytic, Assumptions, Direction, GenerateConditions, Method, PerformanceGoal
