GoodmanKruskalGamma

  • GoodmanKruskalGamma[v1,v2] gives the Goodman – Kruskal γ coefficient for the vectors v1 and v2.
  • GoodmanKruskalGamma[m] gives the Goodman – Kruskal γ coefficients for the matrix m.
  • GoodmanKruskalGamma[m1,m2] gives the Goodman – Kruskal γ coefficients for the matrices m1 and m2.
  • GoodmanKruskalGamma[dist] gives the γ coefficient matrix for the multivariate symbolic distribution dist.
  • GoodmanKruskalGamma[dist,i,j] gives the (i,j)th γ coefficient for the multivariate symbolic distribution dist.

Attributes: Protected, ReadProtected

Symbol has no options.
