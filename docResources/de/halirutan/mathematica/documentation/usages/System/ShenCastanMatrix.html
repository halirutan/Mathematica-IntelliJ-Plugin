ShenCastanMatrix

  • ShenCastanMatrix[r] gives a matrix that corresponds to an exponential kernel of radius r.
  • ShenCastanMatrix[{r, σ }] gives a matrix corresponding to an exponential kernel with radius r and region of support specified by σ .
  • ShenCastanMatrix[r,{n1,n2}] gives a matrix formed from the n1th derivative of the exponential with respect to rows and the n2th derivative with respect to columns.
  • ShenCastanMatrix[r,{{n11,n12},{n21,n22}, … }] gives a matrix formed from the sums of the ni1 and ni2 derivatives.
  • ShenCastanMatrix[{{r1,r2, … }, σ }, … ] gives an array corresponding to an exponential kernel with radius ri in the ith index direction.

Attributes: Protected, ReadProtected

Options: WorkingPrecision, Standardization
