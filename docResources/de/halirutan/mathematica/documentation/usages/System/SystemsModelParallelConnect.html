SystemsModelParallelConnect

  • SystemsModelParallelConnect[sys1,sys2] connects the systems models sys1 and sys2 in parallel.
  • SystemsModelParallelConnect[sys1,sys2,{{in11,in21}, … },{{out11,out21}, … }] connects the inputs in1i to inputs in2i and sums the outputs out1k and outputs out2k.

Attributes: Protected, ReadProtected

Symbol has no options.
