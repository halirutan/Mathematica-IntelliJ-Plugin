BesselFilterModel

  • BesselFilterModel[n] designs a lowpass Bessel filter of order n and cutoff frequency 1.
  • BesselFilterModel[{n, ω c}] uses the cutoff frequency ω c.
  • BesselFilterModel[{n, ω c},var] expresses the model in terms of the variable var.

Attributes: Protected, ReadProtected

Symbol has no options.
