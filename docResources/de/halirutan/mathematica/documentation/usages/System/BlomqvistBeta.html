BlomqvistBeta

  • BlomqvistBeta[v1,v2] gives Blomqvist's medial correlation coefficient β for the vectors v1 and v2.
  • BlomqvistBeta[m] gives Blomqvist's medial correlation coefficient β for the matrix m.
  • BlomqvistBeta[m1,m2] gives Blomqvist's medial correlation coefficient β for the matrices m1 and m2.
  • BlomqvistBeta[dist] gives the medial correlation coefficient matrix for the multivariate symbolic distribution dist.
  • BlomqvistBeta[dist,i,j] gives the (i,j)th medial correlation coefficient for the multivariate symbolic distribution dist.

Attributes: Protected, ReadProtected

Symbol has no options.
