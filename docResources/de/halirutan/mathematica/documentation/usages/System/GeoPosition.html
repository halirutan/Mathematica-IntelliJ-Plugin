GeoPosition

  • GeoPosition[{ ϕ , λ }] represents a geodetic position with latitude ϕ and longitude λ .
  • GeoPosition[{ ϕ , λ ,h}] represents a geodetic position with height h relative to the reference ellipsoid.
  • GeoPosition[{ ϕ , λ ,h},datum] represents a geodetic position referring to the specified datum.

Attributes: Protected, ReadProtected

Symbol has no options.
