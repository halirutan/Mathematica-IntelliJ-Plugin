WignerD

  • WignerD[{j,m1,m2}, ψ , θ , ϕ ] gives the Wigner D-function D m1,m2 j ( ψ , θ , ϕ ).
  • WignerD[{j,m1,m2}, θ , ϕ ] gives the Wigner D-function D m1,m2 j (0, θ , ϕ ).
  • WignerD[{j,m1,m2}, θ ] gives the Wigner D-function D m1,m2 j (0, θ ,0).
