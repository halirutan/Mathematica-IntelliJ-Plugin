HoeffdingD

  • HoeffdingD[v1,v2] gives Hoeffding's dependence measure 𝒟 for the vectors v1 and v2.
  • HoeffdingD[m] gives Hoeffding's dependence measure 𝒟 for the matrix m.
  • HoeffdingD[m1,m2] gives Hoeffding's dependence measure 𝒟 for the matrices m1 and m2.
  • HoeffdingD[dist] gives Hoeffding's 𝒟 matrix for the multivariate symbolic distribution dist.
  • HoeffdingD[dist,i,j] gives the (i,j)th element of 𝒟 for the multivariate symbolic distribution dist.

Attributes: Protected, ReadProtected
