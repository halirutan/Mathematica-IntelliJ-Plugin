SARMAProcess

  • SARMAProcess[{a1, … ,ap},{b1, … ,bq},{s,{ α 1, … , α m},{ β 1, … , β r}},v] represents a seasonal integrated autoregressive moving-average process with ARMA coefficients ai and bj, seasonal order s, seasonal ARMA coefficients α i and β j, and normal white noise with variance v.
  • SARMAProcess[{a1, … ,ap},{b1, … ,bq},{s,{ α 1, … , α m},{ β 1, … , β r}}, Σ ] represents a vector SARMA process driven by normal white noise, with covariance matrix Σ .
  • SARMAProcess[{a1, … ,ap},{b1, … ,bq},{{s1, … },{ α 1, … , α m},{ β 1, … , β r}}, Σ ] represents a vector SARMA process with multiple seasonal orders si.
