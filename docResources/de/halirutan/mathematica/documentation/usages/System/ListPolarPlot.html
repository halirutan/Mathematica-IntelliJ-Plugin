ListPolarPlot

  • ListPolarPlot[{r1,r2, … }] plots points equally spaced in angle at radii ri.
  • ListPolarPlot[{{ θ 1,r1},{ θ 2,r2}, … }] plots points at polar coordinates θ i, ri.
  • ListPolarPlot[{list1,list2, … }] plots several lists of values.
