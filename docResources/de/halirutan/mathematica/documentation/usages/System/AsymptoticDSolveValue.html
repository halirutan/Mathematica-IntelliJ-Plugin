AsymptoticDSolveValue

  • AsymptoticDSolveValue[eqn,f,{x,x0,n}] computes an asymptotic approximation to the differential equation eqn for f[x] centered at x0 of order n.
  • AsymptoticDSolveValue[{eqn1,eqn2, … },{f1,f2, … },{x,x0,n}] computes an asymptotic approximation to a system of differential equations.
  • AsymptoticDSolveValue[eqn,f,x,{ ϵ , ϵ 0,n}] computes an asymptotic approximation of f[x, ϵ ] for the parameter ϵ centered at ϵ 0 of order n.
