InverseFourierSequenceTransform

  • InverseFourierSequenceTransform[expr, ω ,n] gives the inverse discrete-time Fourier transform of expr.
  • InverseFourierSequenceTransform[expr,{ ω 1, ω 2, … },{n1,n2, … }] gives the multidimensional inverse Fourier sequence transform.

Attributes: Protected, ReadProtected

Options: FourierParameters, GenerateConditions, Assumptions
