GradientFilter

  • GradientFilter[image,r] gives an image corresponding to the magnitude of the gradient of image, computed using discrete derivatives of a Gaussian of pixel radius r.
  • GradientFilter[image,{r, σ }] uses a Gaussian with standard deviation σ .
  • GradientFilter[image,{{r1,r2}, … }] uses a Gaussian with radii ri etc. in the vertical and horizontal directions.
  • GradientFilter[data, … ] applies gradient filtering to an array of data.

Attributes: Protected, ReadProtected

Options: NonMaxSuppression, Method, Padding, WorkingPrecision
