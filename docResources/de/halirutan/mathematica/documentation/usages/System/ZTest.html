ZTest

  • ZTest[data] tests whether the mean of the data is zero.
  • ZTest[{data1,data2}] tests whether the means of data1 and data2 are equal.
  • ZTest[dspec, σ ] tests for zero or equal means assuming a population variance σ .
  • ZTest[dspec, σ , μ 0] tests the mean against μ 0.
  • ZTest[dspec, σ , μ 0,"property"] returns the value of "property".

Attributes: Protected, ReadProtected

Options: AlternativeHypothesis, Method, SignificanceLevel, VerifyTestAssumptions
