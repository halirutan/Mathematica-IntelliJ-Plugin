ImageRotate

  • ImageRotate[image] rotates image counterclockwise by 90 ° .
  • ImageRotate[image,side] rotates image to make the top of the image be on the specified side.
  • ImageRotate[image,side1->side2] rotates image to make side1 be on side2.
  • ImageRotate[image, θ ] rotates image counterclockwise by θ radians.
  • ImageRotate[image,side,size] gives an image of the specified size.

Attributes: Protected

Options: Background, Resampling
