BinormalDistribution

  • BinormalDistribution[{ μ 1, μ 2},{ σ 1, σ 2}, ρ ] represents a bivariate normal distribution with mean { μ 1, μ 2} and covariance matrix {{ σ 12, ρ σ 1 σ 2},{ ρ σ 1 σ 2, σ 22}}.
  • BinormalDistribution[{ σ 1, σ 2}, ρ ] represents a bivariate normal distribution with zero mean.
  • BinormalDistribution[ ρ ] represents a bivariate normal distribution with zero mean and covariance matrix {{1, ρ },{ ρ ,1}}.
