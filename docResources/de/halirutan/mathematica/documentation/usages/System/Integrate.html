Integrate

  • Integrate[f,x] gives the indefinite integral ∫ f dx.
  • Integrate[f,{x,xmin,xmax}] gives the definite integral ∫ xmin xmax f dx.
  • Integrate[f,{x,xmin,xmax},{y,ymin,ymax}, … ] gives the multiple integral ∫ xmin xmax dx ∫ ymin ymax d ​ y … f.
