DerivativeFilter

  • DerivativeFilter[image,{n1,n2}] computes the nith derivative of image in the vertical and horizontal directions.
  • DerivativeFilter[image,{n1,n2}, σ ] computes the derivative at a Gaussian scale of standard deviation σ .
  • DerivativeFilter[array,{n1,n2, … }] computes the derivative of array.

Attributes: Protected, ReadProtected

Options: InterpolationOrder, Method, Padding
