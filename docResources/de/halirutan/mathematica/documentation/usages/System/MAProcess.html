MAProcess

  • MAProcess[{b1, … ,bq},v] represents a moving-average process of order q with normal white noise that has variance v.
  • MAProcess[{b1, … ,bq}, Σ ] represents a vector moving-average process with multinormal white noise that has covariance matrix Σ .
  • MAProcess[tproc,q] gives a moving-average representation of the time series process tproc of order q.

Attributes: Protected, ReadProtected
