GARCHProcess

  • GARCHProcess[ κ ,{ α 1, ... , α q},{ β 1, ... , β p}] represents a generalized autoregressive conditionally heteroscedastic process of orders p and q, driven by a standard white noise.
  • GARCHProcess[ κ ,{ α 1, ... , α q},{ β 1, ... , β p},init] represents a GARCH process with initial data init.
