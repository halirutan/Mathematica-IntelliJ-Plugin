NArgMax

  • NArgMax[f,x] gives a position xmax at which f is numerically maximized.
  • NArgMax[f,{x,y, … }] gives a position {xmax,ymax, … } at which f is numerically maximized.
  • NArgMax[{f,cons},{x,y, … }] gives a position at which f is numerically maximized subject to the constraints cons.

Attributes: Protected, ReadProtected

Options: AccuracyGoal, EvaluationMonitor, MaxIterations, Method, PrecisionGoal, StepMonitor, WorkingPrecision
