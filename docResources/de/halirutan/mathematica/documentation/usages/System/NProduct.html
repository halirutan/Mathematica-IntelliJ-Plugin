NProduct

  • NProduct[f,{i,imin,imax}] gives a numerical approximation to the product Underoverscript[ ∏ i=iminimax]f.
  • NProduct[f,{i,imin,imax,di}] uses a step di in the product.

Attributes: HoldAll, Protected

Options: AccuracyGoal, Compiled, EvaluationMonitor, Method, NProductFactors, PrecisionGoal, VerifyConvergence, WorkingPrecision
