CompleteGraph

  • CompleteGraph[n] gives the complete graph with n vertices Kn.
  • CompleteGraph[{n1,n2, … ,nk}] gives the complete k-partite graph with n1+n2+ ⋯ +nk vertices Kn1,n2, … ,nk.

Attributes: NHoldAll, Protected, ReadProtected
