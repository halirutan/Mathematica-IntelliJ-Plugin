AsymptoticIntegrate

  • AsymptoticIntegrate[f,x,{x,x0,n}] computes an asymptotic approximation of the indefinite integral ∫ f(x) ⅆ x for x centered at x0 of order n.
  • AsymptoticIntegrate[f,{x,a,b},{ α , α 0,n}]] computes an asymptotic approximation of the definite integral ∫ a( α ) b( α ) f(x, α ) ⅆ x for α centered at α 0 of order n.
