MultiplicativeOrder

  • MultiplicativeOrder[k,n] gives the multiplicative order of k modulo n, defined as the smallest integer m such that km ≡ 1mod n.
  • MultiplicativeOrder[k,n,{r1,r2, … }] gives the generalized multiplicative order of k modulo n, defined as the smallest integer m such that km ≡ rimod n for some i.
