FourierSequenceTransform

  • FourierSequenceTransform[expr,n, ω ] gives the Fourier sequence transform of expr.
  • FourierSequenceTransform[expr,{n1,n2, … },{ ω 1, ω 2, … }] gives the multidimensional Fourier sequence transform.

Attributes: Protected, ReadProtected

Options: FourierParameters, GenerateConditions, Assumptions
