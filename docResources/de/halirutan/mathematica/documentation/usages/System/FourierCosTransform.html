FourierCosTransform

  • FourierCosTransform[expr,t, ω ] gives the symbolic Fourier cosine transform of expr.
  • FourierCosTransform[expr,{t1,t2, … },{ ω 1, ω 2, … }] gives the multidimensional Fourier cosine transform of expr.

Attributes: Protected, ReadProtected

Options: Assumptions, GenerateConditions, FourierParameters
