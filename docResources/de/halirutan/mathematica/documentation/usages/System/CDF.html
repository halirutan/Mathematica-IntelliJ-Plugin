CDF

  • CDF[dist,x] gives the cumulative distribution function for the symbolic distribution dist evaluated at x.
  • CDF[dist,{x1,x2, … }] gives the multivariate cumulative distribution function for the symbolic distribution dist evaluated at {x1,x2, … }.
  • CDF[dist] gives the CDF as a pure function.

Attributes: Protected, ReadProtected

Symbol has no options.
