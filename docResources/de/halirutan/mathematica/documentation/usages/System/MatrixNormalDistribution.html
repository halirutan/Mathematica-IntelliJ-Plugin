MatrixNormalDistribution

  • MatrixNormalDistribution[ Σ row, Σ col] represents zero mean matrix normal distribution with row covariance matrix Σ row and column covariance matrix Σ col.
  • MatrixNormalDistribution[ μ , Σ row, Σ col] represents matrix normal distribution with mean matrix μ .

Attributes:Protected, ReadProtected

Symbol has no options.
