AggregationLayer

  • AggregationLayer[f] represents a layer that aggregates a tensor of arbitrary rank into a vector, using the function f.
  • AggregationLayer[f,n] aggregates a tensor at level n.
  • AggregationLayer[f,n1;;n2] aggregates a tensor at levels n1 through n2.
  • AggregationLayer[f,{n1,n2, … }] aggregates a tensor at levels n1,n2, … .

Attributes:Protected, ReadProtected

Symbol has no options.
