KochCurve

  • KochCurve[n] gives the line segments representing the nth-step Koch curve.
  • KochCurve[n,{ θ 1, θ 2, … }] takes a series of steps of unit length at successive relative angles θ i.
  • KochCurve[n,{{r1, θ 1},{r2, θ 2}, … }] takes successive steps of lengths proportional to ri.

Attributes:Protected, ReadProtected

Symbol has no options.
