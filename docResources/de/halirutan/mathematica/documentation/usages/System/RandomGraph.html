RandomGraph

  • RandomGraph[{n,m}] gives a pseudorandom graph with n vertices and m edges.
  • RandomGraph[{n,m},k] gives a list of k pseudorandom graphs.
  • RandomGraph[gdist, … ] samples from the random graph distribution gdist.
