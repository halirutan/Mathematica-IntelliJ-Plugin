CentralMoment

  • CentralMoment[list,r] gives the rth central moment of the elements in list with respect to their mean.
  • CentralMoment[dist,r] gives the rth central moment of the symbolic distribution dist.
  • CentralMoment[r] represents the rth formal central moment.

Attributes: NHoldAll, Protected, ReadProtected

Symbol has no options.
