LaplacianGaussianFilter

  • LaplacianGaussianFilter[image,r] convolves image with a Laplacian of Gaussian kernel of pixel radius r.
  • LaplacianGaussianFilter[image,{r, σ }] convolves image with a Laplacian of Gaussian kernel of radius r and standard deviation σ .
  • LaplacianGaussianFilter[data, … ] applies Laplacian of Gaussian filtering to an array of data.

Attributes: Protected, ReadProtected

Options: Standardization, Method, Padding, WorkingPrecision
