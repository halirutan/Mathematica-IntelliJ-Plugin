DeBruijnGraph

  • DeBruijnGraph[m,n] gives the n-dimensional De Bruijn graph with m symbols.
  • DeBruijnGraph[m,n,type] gives the De Bruijn graph with connectivity given by type.

Attributes: NHoldAll, Protected, ReadProtected
