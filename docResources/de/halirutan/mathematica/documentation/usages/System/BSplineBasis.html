BSplineBasis

  • BSplineBasis[d,x] gives the zeroth uniform B-spline basis function of degree d at x.
  • BSplineBasis[d,n,x] gives the nth uniform B-spline basis function of degree d.
  • BSplineBasis[{d,{u1,u2, … }},n,x] gives the nth non-uniform B-spline basis function of degree d with knots at positions ui.

Attributes: NumericFunction, Protected, ReadProtected

Symbol has no options.
