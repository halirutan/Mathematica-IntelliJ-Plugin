PolarPlot

  • PolarPlot[r,{ θ , θ min, θ max}] generates a polar plot of a curve with radius r as a function of angle θ .
  • PolarPlot[{f1,f2, … },{ θ , θ min, θ max}] makes a polar plot of curves with radius functions f1, f2, … .
