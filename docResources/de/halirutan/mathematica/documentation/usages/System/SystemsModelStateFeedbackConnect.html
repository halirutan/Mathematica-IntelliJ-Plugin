SystemsModelStateFeedbackConnect

  • SystemsModelStateFeedbackConnect[ssm,controller] connects the states of StateSpaceModel ssm to controller and the outputs of controller to the inputs of ssm in feedback.
  • SystemsModelStateFeedbackConnect[ssm,controller,{s1, … },{{in1,ftype1}, … }] connects state si of sys1 to the ith input of controller and the jth output of controller to input inj of sys1 with feedback type ftypej.

Attributes: Protected, ReadProtected

Symbol has no options.
