CoxModelFit

  • CoxModelFit[{e1, … ,en}] constructs a model of the baseline hazard h0(t) for events times ei.
  • CoxModelFit[{{{ ξ 11, … , ξ 1p}, … ,{ ξ n   1, … , ξ np}},{e1, … ,en}},{f1, … ,fm},{x1, … ,xp}] constructs a Cox model of the form h0(t) exp( β 1 f1+ … + β m fm), where the fi depend on the xk.

Attributes: Protected, ReadProtected

Options: ConfidenceLevel, ConfidenceRange, ConfidenceTransform, Method, WorkingPrecision, NominalVariables, StrataVariables
