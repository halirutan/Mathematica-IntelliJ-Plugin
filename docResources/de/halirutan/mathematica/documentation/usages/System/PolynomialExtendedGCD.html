PolynomialExtendedGCD

  • PolynomialExtendedGCD[poly1,poly2,x] gives the extended GCD of poly1 and poly2 treated as univariate polynomials in x.
  • PolynomialExtendedGCD[poly1,poly2,x,Modulus->p] gives the extended GCD over the integers mod prime p.

Attributes: Protected

Options: Method, Modulus
